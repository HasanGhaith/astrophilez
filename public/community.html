Math Arena

A competitive math challenge platform built for Astrophiles members. Solve problems, earn ELO rating, and climb the global leaderboard — across four unique game modes.

The ELO Rating System

Every answer you give moves your ELO rating — up for correct, down for wrong. Your score reflects your real skill level and updates after every single answer. Everyone starts at 1500. Where you go from there is up to you.

Correct answer → ELO goes up
Wrong answer → ELO goes down
Harder difficulty = bigger ELO swings

Four Game Modes

Each mode offers a different kind of challenge. Pick what fits your mood and let the ELO decide your rank.

∞

Classic

Ranked

The core experience. Pick a difficulty and answer an endless stream of problems. Every answer — right or wrong — adjusts your ELO. Perfect for grinding your rating or practicing at your own pace with no time pressure.

Unlimited problems Full ELO rewards All 4 difficulties
⚡

Blitz

Timed

60 seconds on the clock. Answer as many problems as you can before time runs out. A live countdown bar turns red when you're almost out of time. Speed and accuracy both matter — find your rhythm fast.

60 second timer Live countdown bar High pressure
📅

Daily Challenge

Daily

Five problems, once a day, one attempt only. Every member gets the exact same set — same problems, same order. Compare your score with the community and see who mastered the day. Resets at midnight.

5 problems One attempt only Resets at midnight
❤️

Survival

Endless

You have three lives. Each wrong answer costs one. Answer correctly and survive as long as possible — no time limit, just you and the problems. How far can you go before you fall?

3 lives No time limit Every mistake counts
